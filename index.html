What?

“Without smart people, there are no smart cities.”

We need people to help us cultivate the smartness of our beloved city. So we're calling upon all thinkers, dreamers, builders, designers, coders, pixelpushers, solder artists, data refiners.

community is the basis upon which innovation is built.

We give these people shared infrastructure they can use to share and exchange ideas because

Good ideas need to be built before they can be tested, building takes time and effort Lab9k will secure funding to compensate the hard work done by the participants. At the moment, we're looking for students to work for us, since studentcontracts are flexible enough to support this model.

Focus

In 2017 we'll dedicate ourselves to Ethereum, Blockchain and IoT

Who?

Lab9K is an initiative of Digipolis. We manage, buy or build all IT-projects of the City of Ghent.

Where?

First floor at the Nest in Ghent.
